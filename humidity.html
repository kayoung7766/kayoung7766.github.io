Humidity

There is no correlation between latitude and humidity. When this analysis was conducted many cities were experiencing high levels of humidity. More cities in the Northern Hemisphere (positive latitude) were experiencing high humidity than the Southern Hemisphere (negative latitude). This is probably due to the fact that this analysis was conducted when the earth's tilt causes it to be summer in the Northern Hemisphere and winter in the Southern Hemisphere.
