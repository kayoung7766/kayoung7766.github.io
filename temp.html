Max Temperature

As expected, cities that are closer to the equator, 0 degrees Latitude, have higher temperatures. This analzsis was conducted in the summer when the earth's tilt causes it to be summer in the Northern Hemisphere and winter in the Southern Hemisphere. Therefore, cities in the Northern Hemisphere (positive latitude) tend to have higher temperatures than than cities with the same absolute value of latitude in the Southern Hemisphere (negative latitude).
